Leatherneck Overland

Nico Reynoso

October 17, 2021


Project Overview:

  • My application is going to be a website for my parents who are avid travelers that have started their own YouTube channel to document their trips. They are doing a lot to be able to put out content of their vacations and the process for their viewers and it's inspiring. The purpose of this site is to organize all of their content into one cohesive space and to display it well.
  • The intended users of this website are people that are interested in traveling to the places that my parents have visited or learning about overlanding in general
  • The content of this website will be a collection of my parents videos and pictures from their travels, an about page that will tell the users a history of how my parents got to where they are, a background on overlanding, the equipment they use and the upgrades they've made to their vehicle that allow for their unique style of traveling, as well as their itineraries from their trips.

Client(s) Information:
  • Christopher & Evelyn Reynoso
  • The Reynoso's are associated with their brand, Leatherneck Overland, that revolves around their travels.
  • Christopher's email address: eccnrey@yahoo.com

Wireframe:
Site Map:
Page Design:
  • Home page
  • This page will introduce Leatherneck Overland and show images from their travels to draw the attention of the user
  • The audience of this page wil be anybody who visits the site as it is the home page
  • There will be a small image gallery, links to socials, links to the other pages, as well as a short introduction
  • About page
  • The purpose of the About Us page will be to inform the users of my clients' past and what has led them to where they are today. It will be a simple, short biography where the users can learn some information about the people traveling the globe.
  • The audience of this page would be anybody that wants to know about who Leatherneck Overland consists of and details about their lives.
  • The content will just be information about my clients and their brand
  • Overlanding page
  • The purpose of this page is to inform the users about what exactly overlanding is and why my clients decided to begin doing it
  • The audience of this page will be anyone interested in overlanding and that specific style of traveling
  • The content of this page will be just a background on overlanding and what it is all about
  • Our Equipment page
  • The purpose of this page is to inform the users about the exact equipment that my clients use for their overlanding
  • The audience of this page is anybody that is interested in some of the highest quality equipment that can be obtained to begin overlanding
  • The content of this page will be some images of my clients equipment and what exactly it is used for so that the users know what it is that they do
  • Itineraries page
  • The purpose of this page is so that the users are able to see my clients' specific itineraries in case they're planning a trip to the same location(s)
  • The audience of this page will be anyone interested in my clients' exact plans of their trips so that they can make sure to knock out major activities
  • The content of this page will just be the itineraries that my clients created for their trips that they've gone on
  • Contact Us page
  • The purpose of this page is for the users to have the ability to contact Leatherneck Overland
  • The audience of this page will be any users that are interested in contacting my clients for several different reasons
  • The content of this page will be a form that users can fill out with a drop down menu of multiple options for their reason of contacting Leatherneck Overland and an area for them to send a message
Dynamic Functionality on the Website:

I will implement a photo gallery on the home page that will display images from my clients' trips around the globe as a way to draw the user in. I also plan on including a form for the user to fill out on the Contact Us page that will allow the user to send a message to Leatherneck Overland and choose a topic from a dropdown menu. I feel that this is will be a very informational site for their brand and allow the user to really undersatnd what their brand is, who they are, the equipment they use, and an ability to reach out to them for several different options.
